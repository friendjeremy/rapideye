GABRIEL KONKLE

OWNER




Ever since playing make-believe in my childhood home in rural Ohio, stories have been my passion. From music to painting to creative writing, they have always been my object, no matter the avenue.

Film captured my heart early on. There's nothing quite so powerful as cinema. It has an unmatched ability to communicate.

Since then, I've done professional photography and videography, as well as pursue my own creative projects, including a feature film, REM, which is in pre-production.

No matter what I've done, though, stories have followed, and I've followed them.
